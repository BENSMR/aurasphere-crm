**Q: Can I switch plans anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on next billing cycle for downgrades, immediately for upgrades.

A: You'll see a warning at 80% usage. At 100%, you can't create new jobs until next month or upgrade plan.

**Q: Can I share my account with family?**
A: No. Each user needs their own account. Use Team plan to add additional users properly.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?**
A: Yes! Pay annually and save 20%. Example: Solo $95.88/year instead of $119.88.

**Q: What if I don't use all 7 days of the trial?**
A: You can start your paid plan anytime. Unused trial days are forfeited.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer 7-day money-back guarantee. After that, no refunds (but you can cancel anytime).

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Stripe (cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay) and Paddle (cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay).

**Q: How often does billing occur?**
A: Monthly (auto-renews) or Annual (12-month commitment with 20% discount).
